Smart City

Status: Completed

Development of IT solutions to optimizse the energy system of of a smart city with application of machine learning methods to reduce harmful emissions. The following will be developed decision making tools to optimize the CHP, heating system of the city and the control system of resource resource consumption of an apartment building.
